Form Elements

This prototype kit will save any information entered in to forms in the browser's local storage as long as some simple steps are followed.

1. The form

Your information will be stored in chunks which will need the be identified. Use the "name" attribute of your form tags to name the sections. For example, a form collecting personal information:

<form name="personal-information">
    <!-- Your form fields will goes here -->
</form>

Your details

Text inputs

Like with the form tag, we use the name attribute to give our chunks of information an identifier. We will also use the same identifier to link our labels with their corresponding fields.

The example below uses 'full-name' as the identifier for this field.

<label class="form-label-bold" for="full-name">Full name</label>
<p class="form-hint">As shown on your birth certificate or passport</p>
<input type="text" class="form-control" id="full-name" name="full-name" />

As shown on your birth certificate or passport

Date inputs

There is styling ready for text fields being used for date inputs. Below is an example of a Date of Birth field, using the field names dob-day, dob-month and dob-year.

The css classes below (form-date, form-group-day, form-group-month, form-group-year) are important for styling.

<div class="form-group">
  <fieldset>
    <legend class="form-label-bold">Date of birth</legend>
    <div class="form-date">
      <p class="form-hint">For example, 31 3 1980</p>
      <div class="form-group form-group-day">
        <label for="dob-day">Day</label> <input class="form-control" id="dob-day" type="number" pattern="[0-9]*" min="0" max="31" name="dob-day" />
      </div>
      <div class="form-group form-group-month">
        <label for="dob-month">Month</label> <input class="form-control" id="dob-month" type="number" pattern="[0-9]*" min="0" max="12" name="dob-month" />
      </div>
      <div class="form-group form-group-year">
        <label for="dob-year">Year</label> <input class="form-control" id="dob-year" type="number" pattern="[0-9]*" min="0" max="2014" name="dob-year" />
      </div>
    </div>
  </fieldset>
</div>
