CAMHS Bedfinder A 24 hour prototype for Child and Adolescent Mental Health Service

The CAMHS Bedfinder tackles a problem that will be familiar, not only to those in the CAMHS but across the NHS. This is the problem of finding an empty bed for a patient in a situation when places are scarce.

The prototype was developed during the two day NHS 'hackathon'. During this time The Guardian published this article illustrating the seriousness of this solvable problem.
